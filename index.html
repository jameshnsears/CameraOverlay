Privacy Policy

Free, open-source software

  • The "Camera Overlay" App is free open-source software: the source code can be found at github.com/jameshnsears/CemeraOverlay

Variants

There are two variants of the App; they are identified with the following in their version name:

  • -googleplay
  • -fdroid

The "-googleplay" variant is deployed on Google Play and "-fdroid" on F-Droid.

Only the "-googleplay" variant uses Analytics.


Analytics

  • All events recorded in analytics by the App are anonymous
  • No photo or location data is recorded in analytics by the App

Analytic Systems

Analytics are recorded by the following, Google owned & maintained, systems:

  1. Google Play
  2. Firebase Crashlytics
  3. Google Analytics

Please contact james.hn.sears@gmail.com for all issues relating to this privacy policy.
